Basic Scrum

3 roles:
- Product Owner: list tasks, create & assign tasks, decide the priorities tasks base on client’s requirements.
- Scrum Master: control team with all tasks are effective and meet the deadlines.
- Team (Dev team): software engineers, testers, writer, PM, etc.

1 sprint (1-3 weeks)
- Sprint: Plan, build, test and review
=> Deliverable Product

Product Backlog: saving user stories(tasks) from Product Owner
Sprint Backlog: saving some tasks that need to do in next sprint
User stories: Tasks descriptions (As a …, I need to do …., so that ……)

3 Meetings:
- Sprint planning: Meeting before starting a sprint with tasks from Product Backlog
    - Estimate: how many people do to do? How many stories point  
- Daily stand up/ Daily meeting: Meeting after creating sprint and sync information of the team( done, in progress and todo)
- Sprint Review/Restrospective: Normally, meeting at the last day of sprint for catching up and reviewing with some things done from the sprint.
